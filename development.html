Description

Welcome to Dimple Tower at Constitution Avenue, Central Area to be operated as Campbell Gray Hotel, The Dimple Tower Pioneer project in Abuja, Nigeria and our first offering in this high demand district. Hailed as the region’s business capital, Constitution Avenue has fast become a hub for prime residential and commercial developments, thanks to its prime location between important road networks, Independence Avenue and Herbert Macaulay Road. Its convenient distance from some of the city’s top business and leisure landmarks makes it easy to see why Constitution Avenue attracts such major investment.

A short drive takes you to Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport, while World Trade Centre, American Embassy and the Russian Embassy are situated just 2km away.

This exciting 4-star hotel is less than 2km from Abuja Metro Station, a major railway project that links Constitution Avenue to the International Airport. The highly anticipated development, which will open October 2021, will add to the demand for high quality hotel developments in the area.

With its superior facilities and pivotal location, Dimple Tower at Constitution Avenue is already poised to bring investors top returns.
